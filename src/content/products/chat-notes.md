## What it does

Type or paste a conversation — meeting notes, Slack threads, interview transcripts — and Chat Notes structures it into clean, searchable notes with key takeaways highlighted.

## Why I built it

After every meeting I'd have a wall of unstructured text. Extracting action items and decisions was a manual slog. I wanted something that takes the raw dump and gives back something useful in seconds.

## How it works

- **Paste or type** — drop in any conversation text
- **AI summarization** — extracts key points, action items, and decisions
- **Export** — Markdown, PDF, or copy to clipboard
