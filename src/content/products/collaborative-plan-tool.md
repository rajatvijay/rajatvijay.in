## What it does

A shared planning surface for small teams. Create plans, assign tasks, track progress — all in real-time with no accounts required. Share a link and start collaborating.

## Why I built it

Every project management tool requires a signup, an onboarding flow, and a decision about which plan to pick. For quick team planning sessions, I wanted something that works in 5 seconds.
