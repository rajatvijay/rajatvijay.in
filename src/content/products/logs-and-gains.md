## What it does

A fitness tracker built for people who want to log and move on — not navigate 47 tabs. Track workouts, nutrition, and body measurements with a clean interface that loads instantly.

## Why I built it

Every fitness app I tried was either too simple (just a timer) or too complex (social feeds, gamification, AI coaches). I wanted something in between: structured enough to track progress, minimal enough to not become a chore.

## How it works

- **Workout logging** — tap to start, log sets as you go, done
- **Nutrition tracking** — quick-add with a barcode scanner or manual entry
- **Progress charts** — weekly and monthly views for every metric you track
- **Export** — CSV export for your own analysis
