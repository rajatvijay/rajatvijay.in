## What it does

Mind lets you capture anything — thoughts, links, quotes, ideas — from anywhere on your iPhone via a single iOS Shortcut. Everything syncs to a clean web dashboard where you can search, tag, and organize later.

## Why I built it

I was tired of switching between notes apps, bookmarking tools, and read-later services. I wanted one place to dump everything, triggered from the share sheet or a home screen shortcut.

## How it works

- **iOS Shortcut** — share anything to Mind from any app, or quick-capture from the home screen
- **Web dashboard** — search, filter by tag, bulk actions
- **CSV import** — migrate from other tools (mymind, Notion, etc.)
