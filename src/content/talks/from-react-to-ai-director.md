When I told my team I was going to lead our AI initiatives, the first question was: "But you're a frontend engineer?" This talk unpacks why that question misses the point entirely.

I cover the three skills that transferred directly — composability, state management, and user empathy — and share the playbook I used to go from writing React components to directing AI product development at a Series B company, without a traditional ML background.
